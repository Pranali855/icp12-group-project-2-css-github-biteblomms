About Bytebloom

We connect food lovers with the best eateries

Founded in 2010, FoodExpress began as a small startup with a big dream: to revolutionize the way people experience food delivery in urban spaces. What started as a two-person operation is now a nationwide service spanning over 50 cities.

Our platform connects hungry customers with thousands of restaurants, from small family-run eateries to high-end dining establishments. We're not just a delivery service - we're a community of food enthusiasts dedicated to bringing joy to every meal.

With our cutting-edge technology and passionate team, we've made ordering food as simple as a few taps. But behind every order is a carefully crafted system ensuring fresh, hot food arrives at your door in record time.

Our Mission

At BiteBlooms, we believe that great food experiences should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to empower food lovers with reliable information, honest reviews, and seamless discovery tools.

We're committed to supporting local restaurants while helping users find exactly what they're craving, whether it's a hole-in-the-wall gem or a fine dining experience.
